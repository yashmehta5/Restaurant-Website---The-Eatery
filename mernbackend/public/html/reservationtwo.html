What to know before you go

Important dining information

We have a 15 minute grace period. Please call us if you are running later than 15 minutes after your reservation time.

We may contact you about this reservation, so please ensure your email and phone number are up to date....

Your table will be reserved for 1 hour 45 minutes for parties of up to 2; 2 hours for parties of up to 4; 2 hours 30 minutes for parties of up to 6; and 2 hours 45 minutes for parties of 7+.

A note from the restaurant

Cancellation Policy: Cancellations must be done 24 hrs in advance. Cancelling your entire party within 24 hrs or if you no show will result in a charge of Rs.100 per person. Please note that all reservations are dependent on Gurgaon indoor dining mandates. We will contact you in the event that we have to cancel your reservation.
---
We can not accommodate multiple reservations to be seated next to each other. For larger parties please call us directly to speak to our Special Events team
